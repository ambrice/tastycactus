--- title: Timesheet Release 1.3 kind: article created_at: 01/12/2011 project: Timesheet --- Timesheet 1.3 is released in the Android Marketplace. Search for Timesheet or Tasty Cactus. Added the ability to attach comments to a time entry. Also, from the time entries list you can email a time report. Set the default email address in preferences. Timesheet source can be found on [github](http://github.com/ambrice/timesheet).
