--- title: Timesheet Release 1.1 kind: article created_at: 03/18/2010 project: Timesheet --- Timesheet 1.1 is released in the Android Marketplace. Search for Timesheet or Tasty Cactus. Updates: - New option to export timesheet as a CSV to the SD card. Files are written to a timesheets/ directory. - Tasks are now editable once created - Added a preference to specify the start of the work week - Added a preference for whether to display only billable tasks in the weekly view and in the CSV exports.
